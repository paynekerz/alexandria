# Welcome to your Alexandria library

This vault is your personal library of everything Alexandria teaches you.

## How it's organized

- One folder per project you learn about, created automatically at your first saved lesson.
- Inside each project: `_index.md` lists every lesson, `_glossary.md` defines every concept, and `Sessions/` holds one note per lesson.
- `_Concepts/` holds ideas you've met in more than one project.

## How lessons get here

Ask `alexandria-teach` to explain something in your codebase. At the end of every lesson it offers to save; say yes and `alexandria-librarian` files the note, updates the glossary, and keeps the indexes current. Later, `alexandria-recall` finds past lessons before anything gets re-taught — and warns you when the code a lesson covered has changed since.

Nothing is ever saved without your say-so. This library only contains what you chose to keep.
